About Orders

Orders placed on Facebook or Instagram are put on hold during at least the first 30 minutes during which time they are being processed by our internal systems. This delay is also designed to give chance for buyers to cancel their orders in case of remorse. After Facebook has verified that a given transaction is legitimate and the remorse period has elapsed, the corresponding order will be made available for you to fulfill.
